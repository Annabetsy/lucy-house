# 🏡 Lucy's Toca World

A cute, colorful pretend-play game made by Lucy! Dress up characters, decorate
rooms, design your own houses, and visit lots of different places — all drawn in
a soft, flat cartoon style.

## ▶️ How to play

1. Download or open `index.html`.
2. On a computer: **double-click it** to open in your web browser.
3. On a tablet or phone: open the file in the browser, then tap
   **Share → "Add to Home Screen"** so it gets its own app icon. 📱

Everything works with your finger — tap and drag to move things around.

## ✨ What you can do

- 🗺️ **Visit places** — House, Hair Salon, Hospital, School, Mall, Café,
  Pet Shop, Park, Beach, Ice Cream Shop, Bakery, Haunted House, and a Farm.
- 🏢 **6 floors + a backyard** in the house, with a volleyball court and a pool
  with a waterslide.
- 🚪 **Add and design your own rooms** — rename them and paint the walls & floor.
- 👧 **Dress up characters** — hairstyles, hats, glasses, bows, dresses, and colors.
- 🛋️ **Decorate** by dragging in furniture, food, animals, plants, and toys.
- 💬 **Speech bubbles** so characters can talk.
- 💾 **Saves automatically** — your world is always there when you come back.

## 🛠️ How it's made

It's a single `index.html` file — no internet needed to play. All the artwork is
hand-drawn with SVG in a friendly, rounded style. (Original art made in a
Toca-Boca-*inspired* style; Toca Boca is its own company and this is just a fun
homemade project.)
